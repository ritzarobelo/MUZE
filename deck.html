I created this deck as a sister deck to the Golden Thread Tarot - both visually and conceptually. While the Golden Thread is really focused on looking within, the Luminous Spirit is really about radiating outwards. This focus will also be reflected in the app to be created as a companion in the future. I was inspired by the way that we interact with light - how when light travels through a prism, we see all of the colors that are within. The archetypes within the tarot are all within us, and our decks, are the prism that lets all aspects of ourselves speak.
